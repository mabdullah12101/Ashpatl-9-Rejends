Gameplay

With better visuals, more intricate courses, and a wider range of vehicles, it expands on the popularity of its Asphalt series predecessors to provide an even better racing experience.

Game Modes

Career Mode

Players can earn new cars and upgrades in this mode by finishing numerous races, which is a narratively driven progression system. There are goals for every race in career mode, such finishing first, pulling off certain feats, or setting a personal record.

Game Modes

Multiplayer

Players from all over the world can compete against one another in real time in this mode. Players can test their abilities against actual opponents in multiplayer races and go up the worldwide leaderboards.

Game Modes

Events

There are some time-limited activities with exclusive benefits. These events frequently provide distinctive challenges as well as chances to win rare vehicles or merchandise.

Controls

Touchdrive

A simple control scheme where the player concentrates on timing and path selection, leaving the steering to the game. For gamers that prefer to take in the action without obsessing about technical controls, this is perfect.

Controls

Manual Controls

For those who want more control, a traditional control system that lets players steer, brake, and accelerate by hand offers a more hands-on experience.

Race Types

Classic

Standard races in which first place is the objective.

Race Types

Time Attack

Reach the finish line in a race against the time.

Race Types

Hunted

Stay clear of police cruisers and cross the finish line in the allotted amount of time.

Race Types

Slipstream

Gain speed by driving in the slipstream of other cars.

Car Customization and Upgrades

Blueprints

To unlock and improve automobiles, gather blueprints. For each automobile to be upgraded or unlocked, a specific amount of blueprints must be collected.

Car Customization and Upgrades

Customization

Make your automobiles seem different by adding decals, rims, and paint jobs to give them a unique style.

Car Customization and Upgrades

Performance Upgrades

Improve the top speed, acceleration, handling, and nitro efficiency of your cars to enhance performance on the track.
